How it works

Three steps.
Zero distraction.

01

Start a session

Set your focus duration and hit start. Study Sight activates your camera in the background. No setup needed.

02

Get detected

The focus engine watches for real concentration signals: eye contact, posture, attention. Your focus score updates live.

03

Lock in

When your focus score hits 90+ and holds, the timer pauses automatically. You stay in the zone. No interruptions.

Features

Built for students,
by students.

Webcam focus detection

Real-time CV analysis detects whether you're actually studying. Not just whether a timer is running.

Locked In mode

When you're locked in, the timer automatically extends so you stay in the zone.

Focus stats

Track your real study time, not just hours at a desk. See your focus score trends over days and weeks.

Goals & habits

Set daily and weekly study goals. Track streaks, completion rates, and build consistent study habits.
